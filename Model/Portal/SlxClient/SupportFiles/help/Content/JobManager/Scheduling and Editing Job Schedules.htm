Editing Job Schedules

The Schedules tab lists all jobs that have been set to run at a scheduled time and provides information about that schedule. From this tab, you can delete or modify a job schedule.

To delete

  • Select the job, right-click and then click Delete Job.

To modify

1. Select the job, right-click and then click Edit Schedule.

2. In the Run/Schedule Job page, make your modifications.

3. Click OK.
