Report Preview

Your completed reports are stored on the Reports History tab..

You can only see reports that have been released to you or to a team that you belong to. You cannot save reports in the Saleslogix Web Client.

You must allow pop-ups before you can export or print a report.

How Do I?

View or print a detail report from a detail view

View or print at report from the Report History tab
  1. On the Navigation bar, click Reports, and then click the History tab.
  2. Locate the report you want. You can sort by grid column headings.
  3. Click the report.
  4. The report opens in the viewer native to the file type. For example, Acrobat Reader for PDF formats.
Preview a report from the viewer
  1. Select the report you want to preview, then click View Report. The report appears in the Report Viewer. Depending on the report selected and the number of results produced, a tree view may appear on the left of the window. The tree view lists the pages in the report in alphanumeric or chronological order.
  2. To view a specific page of the report, click the plus sign to expand the list, and then click a name in the tree view.
