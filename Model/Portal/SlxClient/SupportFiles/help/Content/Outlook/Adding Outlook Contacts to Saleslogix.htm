Adding Outlook Contacts to Saleslogix

To add Outlook contacts to Saleslogix, you must first create and configure a contact sync group in Saleslogix and set your contact sync options.

To add

  • Add the contacts to the folder you designated as the contact sync folder in your Saleslogix Connector Sync Options.
