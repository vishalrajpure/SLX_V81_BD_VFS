Saleslogix and Outlook

There are several ways that Saleslogix integrates with Outlook.

 Your administrator must configure and grant permission before you can utilize many of the features of Outlook sync and Outlook Integration.
  • Outlook sync - allows you to synchronize your contacts and calendar between Saleslogix and Outlook.
  • Outlook Integration - allows you to save Outlook e-mail messages as Saleslogix history items.

Before you can use Outlook sync or Outlook Integration, you must configure Outlook Sync and Outlook Integration options in Outlook.
